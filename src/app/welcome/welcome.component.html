How is BMI used?

A high BMI can be an indicator of high body fatness. BMI can be used as a
screening tool but is not diagnostic of the body fatness or health of an
individual.

To determine if a high BMI is a health risk, a healthcare provider would
need to perform further assessments. These assessments might include
skinfold thickness measurements, evaluations of diet, physical activity,
family history, and other appropriate health screenings.
